Review Pulse: INDmoney

Week 25 (June 15 – June 21, 2026)

This week saw a significant 14% uptick in positive sentiment following the Dashboard UI Refresh. While overall ratings stabilized at 4.2, we observed a recurring friction point regarding hidden transaction fees which remains the primary driver of 3-star reviews.

Theme: Hidden Charges & Fees

Users are reporting unexpected costs during the US Stock execution phase, specifically around currency conversion spreads that aren't clearly disclosed in the primary UI flow.

Business Impact

High churn risk for first-time investors; 22% of negative reviews this week cited 'transparency' issues.

Action Plan

  • Update the 'Review Order' screen to show a detailed breakdown of conversion fees.
  • Add a tooltip explaining the mid-market rate vs. the execution rate.
  • Send a push notification to affected users with a fee-waiver voucher for their next trade.
